HeartX – Heart Rate Monitor & Pulse Tracker

HeartX helps you accurately measure, track, and monitor your heart rate using your smartphone’s built-in sensor. Whether you are tracking your fitness progress, monitoring your heart health, or simply curious about your pulse, HeartX provides real-time insights with ease.

Key Features:

  • Accurate heart rate measurement using your phone’s sensor
  • Heart rate tracking with detailed history and trends
  • Health insights and charts for better analysis
  • Saved history to access previous measurements anytime
  • Multi-language support for global users
  • User-friendly interface with a simple and intuitive design

Why Monitor Your Heart Rate? Your heart rate is an important indicator of overall health. Regular tracking can help you:

  • Understand how your heart responds to daily activities
  • Monitor stress and fitness levels
  • Maintain better heart health over time
  • Download HeartX today and start tracking your heart health!
